Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my goals?

To change your goals, navigate to the goals section in your settings, and update your preferences.

How do I export my data?

To export your data, go to the settings page, select "Export Data," and choose the desired format. Your data will be downloaded as a file for safekeeping.

How do I connect to a new device?

To connect to a new device, open the settings, go to "Device Connections," and follow the prompts to add a new device.
